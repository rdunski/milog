F.A.Q

Most Asked Questions

How to create a trip log?

Click on the "Trip Logs" button on the left sidebar. Then click the "Create Log" button at the top of the left sidebar. Fill out the appropriate fields and click submit.

*If you do not see a sidebar, click the three lines in the top left corner of the screen next to the MILOG title.

How to add a vehicle?

Click on the "Vehicles" button on the left sidebar. Then click the "Add Vehicle" button at the top of the left sidebar. Give the vehicle a unique name and click submit.

*If you do not see a sidebar, click the three lines in the top left corner of the screen next to the MILOG title.

*NOTE: You cannnot use the same vehicle name twice.

How to update or delete a trip log?

On the "Trip Logs" page simply click on the trashcan button to delete the desired log or click on the pencil button to update the desired log.

How to print total logs?

On the "Trip Logs" page click the submit button where it says "Export to PDF". You can select a specific date range, otherwise it will print all logs.

How to delete account?

Simply click on the "Account" button on the left sidebar and select the button "Delete".

*If you do not see a sidebar, click the three lines in the top left corner of the screen next to the MILOG title.

*NOTE: This is permanent and all data will be lost.
