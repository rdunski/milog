FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can see my logs?

Only you! The app has built in authentication to make sure you and only you have the ability to access your own data.

I accidently put in the wrong numbers and posted incorrect data, what do I do?

Milog is built on its ease of use and one of such being the ability to easily modify your travel logs whether it be online with our website or from the comfort of your phone. All that’s needed is that you select your log and click Edit. Modify data as needed and then save. Your logs will be updated immediately.

I have had an android for a while, and have switched phones to apple and I’m worried about my data not switching over, what should I do?

Milog has built in cloud storage. As long as you have your email and password, there should be no problem on viewing, modifying, or deleting your logs.

I have to run a few errands in between a trip, how do I account for this?

Milog comes with a pause feature that lets you pause in between trips. If for whatever reason you need to pause your trip, all you have to do is press pause and record your current odometer reading. Once you’re ready to un-pause your trip all you have to do is click Un-pause and the trip will resume as it was before.

There was some tolls and fees I had to take care of during my trip, is there a way to add this on to it?

Every log comes with a notes section that lets you write down whatever was noteworthy for you on your trip. Whether it was a simple fine, or just a note on who you met with, it will be attached to the log on which is was written for future viewing.

How long are my logs up for viewing?

One of the reasons Milog was developed was to help with tax documents or other possible company documents that require the verification of travel. The Milog app lists out the most recent logs made while tuning into the website will list all your logs that you have made.
